Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

MEW Shifts ("MEW Shifts", "we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the MEW Shifts mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MEW Shifts is designed to minimize data collection and give users control over their information.

Most work-related data such as schedules, attendance records, overtime information, notes, groups, and preferences are stored securely within the app and associated cloud services only when necessary for enabled features.

If you choose to sign in or use optional cloud-based features, we may collect limited account information through trusted authentication providers, including:

  • Email address
  • Authentication provider identifier
  • Basic profile information provided by the sign-in service

2. How Information Is Used

Information collected by MEW Shifts is used only to provide and improve app functionality.

  • Enable secure account authentication
  • Provide cloud backup and restore functionality
  • Support workforce and group-related features
  • Synchronize user data across devices when enabled
  • Improve app reliability, stability, and performance

We do not sell personal information and do not use personal data for advertising purposes.

3. Workforce & Location Features

Some workforce management features may use optional location-based services, such as geofence attendance functionality.

Location information is only accessed with user permission and only while using features that require location verification.

4. Data Storage & Security

MEW Shifts uses industry-standard security measures to help protect user information.

Cloud-based data is securely stored using trusted infrastructure providers with authentication and access controls designed to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

MEW Shifts may use trusted third-party services to provide authentication, notifications, cloud storage, analytics, and subscription-related functionality.

  • Apple Sign-In
  • Google Sign-In
  • Supabase
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • RevenueCat

These services process information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms.

6. User Control & Account Deletion

Creating an account is optional for most app features.

Users may request account deletion and removal of associated cloud data directly from within the app settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

MEW Shifts is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
